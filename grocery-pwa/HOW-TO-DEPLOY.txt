# Grocery List PWA — Deployment Guide

## What's in this package
- index.html   — the full app
- manifest.json — makes it installable as an app
- sw.js         — service worker (enables offline use)
- icons/        — app icons (192x192 and 512x512)

---

## Step 1 — Create a GitHub account
Go to https://github.com and sign up (free).

---

## Step 2 — Create a new repository
1. Click the "+" button (top right) → "New repository"
2. Name it: grocery-list  (or anything you like)
3. Set it to Public
4. Click "Create repository"

---

## Step 3 — Upload the files
1. In your new repo, click "uploading an existing file"
2. Drag and drop ALL files from this zip:
   - index.html
   - manifest.json
   - sw.js
   - icons/icon-192.png
   - icons/icon-512.png
   
   IMPORTANT: For the icons folder, upload the files into a folder called "icons"
   You can do this by dragging the icons folder directly.

3. Click "Commit changes"

---

## Step 4 — Enable GitHub Pages
1. Go to your repo → Settings tab
2. Scroll down to "Pages" (left sidebar)
3. Under "Source", select: Deploy from a branch
4. Branch: main  |  Folder: / (root)
5. Click Save
6. Wait ~2 minutes, then your app will be live at:
   https://YOUR-USERNAME.github.io/grocery-list/

---

## Step 5 — Install on your Android phone
1. Open Chrome on your Android phone
2. Go to: https://YOUR-USERNAME.github.io/grocery-list/
3. Chrome will show a banner: "Add Grocery List to Home screen" → tap it
   (If no banner appears: tap the 3-dot menu → "Add to Home screen")
4. Tap "Add" on the confirmation dialog
5. The app icon appears on your home screen!

---

## Done!
- Opens full screen, no browser bar
- Works offline after first load
- Data saved locally on your phone
- Share the URL with anyone to use on their device too

---

## Updating the app later
Just edit index.html on GitHub and commit — the app updates automatically.
(You may need to clear the app cache on your phone once: Settings → Apps → Chrome → Clear cache)
